Ebbings

Named after the German psychologist and pioneer of memory research, Hermann Ebbinghaus (1850-1909), Ebbings helps you learn and retain information more efficiently than traditional methods.

Teachers

Use Ebbings to assign decks of information for your students to learn. There are several pre-made decks for English language learning (for native language Chinese students), and other decks can be requested. You can easily keep track of your students' progress.

Students

Study the decks assigned to you by your teacher. Try to study for a few minutes each time in short, frequent bursts in order to maximize benefits.
